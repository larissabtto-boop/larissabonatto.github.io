The Product

Yoroi is a non-custodial browser and mobile crypto wallet for the Cardano ecosystem, used by over 1 million users worldwide. It allows users to securely store assets, manage tokens and NFTs, and stake ADA without running a full node.

The Problem

The wallet creation flow was confusing and failed to clearly communicate one of the most critical concepts: the recovery phrase is the only way to access the wallet. As a result:

  • Around 30% of support tickets were related to users losing their recovery phrase
  • Many users dropped off during wallet creation
  • Security-related copy was unclear and easy to skip
  • Users made mistakes during the phrase confirmation step

My Role

Senior Product Designer focused on onboarding and security-critical flows.

  • Led user research and usability testing
  • Analyzed support data with Customer Success
  • Facilitated ideation workshops and defined new onboarding flows
  • Designed the new recovery phrase experience
  • Partnered closely with Product and Engineering through implementation
  • Participated in QA across platforms

Outcomes

  • Support tickets related to lost recovery phrases dropped from 30% to 10%
  • Wallet creation completion increased by 3% in the first month
  • Drop-off during onboarding decreased by 15%
  • Users reported the flow felt clearer, faster, and more trustworthy
  • Reduced friction for both users and the support team
